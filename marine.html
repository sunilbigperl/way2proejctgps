Marine Industry

Commercial marine operations require constant communication with the home ports and real-time weather alerts for the safety of their crew. Gaps in shore-to-ship communication cannot be left unfilled. Real-time monitoring of vessels and inter-communication among the vessels in essential for information sharing and can even help against theft, especially for small boats and vessels.

Way2GPS Tracking Solutions had developed a solution which makes it easy for the ships and boats to be monitored in real time. Our solution enables you to monitor fuel consumption, engines RPM, and many other operations critical parameters.

  • Establishing better monitoring of the vessel and critical parameters from shore.
  • Guide in rescue operations using GPS location coordinates in time of emergencies.
  • Polygonal geo-fencing of fishing areas in compliance with the maritime border regulations.
  • 24*7 tracking of vessel locations.
  • Constant fuel monitoring of the vessels and optimization of fuel expenditure.
